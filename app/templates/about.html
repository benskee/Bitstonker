About Bitstonker

"Watch my wallet outperform your market."

Thank you for visiting bitstonker.com. I am a software engineer currently in search of a job and this is my portfolio project. This website is intended to show the performance of investing your bitcoin in stocks, I hope you find it useful.

What is bitcoin?
Bitcoin is a decentralized permissionless global cryptocurrency that runs on a blockchain. If that doesn't make sense to you, don't feel bad because most people still don't get it. If you would like to learn more about bitcoin, Peter McCormack made a great beginner's guide on his What Bitcoin Did podcast. What Bitcoin Did: Bitcoin in One Lesson (duration 1hr). If you are feeling more ambitious The Bitcoin Standard by Saifedean Ammous will give you a good foundational knowledge of bitcoin.


What is a sat?
Each bitcoin can be divided into 100,000,000 units called satoshis (sats). This is named after Satoshi Nakamoto the pseudonymous creater of bitcoin.

What is a stonk?
A stonk is a meme used to poke fun at poor investments. This term has grown in popularity in the 2020's as the stock market has continued to rise despite major changes in demand due to the Covid19 pandemic.


How was daily price in bitcoin calculated?
The daily price was calculated using data for the price of bitcoin at 4pm each day to match the time for the closing price of stonk. The historic prices were taken from the Gemini exchange and is kept current via data from Alphavantage. The dates are limited to the beginning of 2016 until today. Sorry, I do not have future dates. If that changes I will be sure to update this from my private island.
